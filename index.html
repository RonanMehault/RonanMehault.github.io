Welcome to Ronan's Page

Ronan has been working professionally in archaeology since 2009, with field and research experience in Northwestern France, and in Eastern and Western Canada. Ronan’s research has focussed primarily upon the recent pre-Contact Period in Northeastern North America that he addressed with the use of evolutionary theories and methodologies. Ronan has taught archaeological courses at university and has trained students in archaeology in the context of the Archaeology Field School at the Université de Montréal. He has also held archaeological research permits in Eastern Canada and has published in national periodicals and international textbooks. Ronan has cofounded and been working for Pathways Archaeological Consulting Ltd. since 2021 as a GIS specialist, field archaeologist, and consultant in Anthropology.

GIST-8117 - Cartography and Geovizualisation - Project 4

The primary objective of this mapping exercise consists of representing the distribution of underwater archaeological sites both in geographical (through their relationship with the European Continental Shelf, using bathymetric data) and chronological terms (by sorting said sites according to their respective chronocultural taxa [Palaeolithic, Mesolithic, Neolithic, Bronze Age, Iron Age, etc.).
